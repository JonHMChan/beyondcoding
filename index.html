About Us

Launched in May 2014, the NYC Tech Talent Pipeline is a $10 million industry partnership designed to deliver quality jobs for our people and quality talent for our businesses. To deliver on this promise, the NYC Tech Talent Pipeline is working with public and private partners to define employer needs, develop training and education solutions, and deliver homegrown talent for 21st century jobs across the five boroughs.

If you, like me, had that life changing experience some time in your childhood where you entered some instructions into a screen and the machine executed them, well then you know the power of coding to make you think differently, make you think more, and endow you with superpowers that others just don't have.

— Fred Wilson, Partner at Union Square Ventures

30 Second Elevator Pitch

Trello

As you may know, an elevator pitch is a short summary used to quickly and simply define a profession, product, service, organization, or event and its value proposition. But, it is also a short summary used to quickly and simply define YOU. How do you explain your value to prospective employers? How do you pitch yourself in casual settings? How do you pitch yourself while on an interview? How do you pitch yourself on social media? How do you pitch yourself to a reporter? How do you pitch yourself to your family? When should you alter your pitch?

Working with Data

Kickstarter

Employing data-driven thinking has become an essential part of working at a tech company. Kickstarter's data team has developed a series of internal courses around data literacy to help newcomers to the field incorporate it into their day-to-day. This session will pull from those courses to give you an overview about how to think critically with data, tell data stories, and best represent it visually. You'll come away with a stronger understanding of the basics of data analysis and how to apply it to common business and technology challenges. No previous experience working with data is expected, but we hope participants will arrive curious and eager to think critically.

Business & Personal Networking: Casting the Net Wide

Crest

Did you know that 60-80% of career and business opportunities are found through meaningful and intentional networking? However we live in a digital age where we assume the number of social media connections infers that we are relevant. Yet, some can bearly get 1% of their connections to respond directly to a poke, inquiries, and a times invitations.

Let’s discuss the what, why, and how to networking. Join me in discovering how to expand your personal and professional network which will increase your net-worth, career opportunities, authentic relationships, and your personal brand without lifting your smartphone and enabling an app. Most importantly, let’s become comfortable entering a room full of unfamiliar faces, putting your best self forward, and discovering that most people you will encounter are in search for the very same thing: connections that counts.
